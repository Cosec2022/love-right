# Love Right v0.5.5 — Consequential Choices

- Consequential choices now look and read differently from ordinary projection choices.
- Story 07 marks three key decisions and one final decision with dedicated narrative notices, visual treatment and meaningful choice subtext.
- The last choice no longer jumps directly into analysis. It first reveals the exact ending the player authored, then invites the player to read what the route says about them.
- Added generic `decisionType`, `decisionLabel`, `decisionNote` and `finalReveal` content fields for future stories.
- Added schema, validator and regression tests for decision scenes and ending reveals.
