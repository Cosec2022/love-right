## Hidden ending decision

`hiddenEnding: null`.

There is no fifth factual future that is not a diluted version of cooperative rebuilding, deliberate distance, truthful separation, or accountable collapse. A “secret perfect touring couple” would be a better-version prize; a secret solo artistic victory would repeat `last-cue`. Multi-choice conditions could be invented, but would not add an independent theme or aftertaste. Not adding one protects the four endings from becoming four ordinary routes plus a supposedly correct answer.
